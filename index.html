Then and Now 2/14/2025

Through every hour, near or far,
You light my world—my guiding star.
Your voice, a warmth I always crave,
A love so deep, so true, so brave.

You work so hard, you give your all,
Yet still, you always take time to call.
With every word, with every touch,
You remind me I am loved so much.

You lift me up, you make me strong,
With you, my heart knows it belongs.
Each day, I grow because you're near,
Your love has made my path so clear.

I'm proud of you in all you do,
The fire, the strength, the heart in you.
Forever grateful, always true,
My love, my world—I'll cherish you.
